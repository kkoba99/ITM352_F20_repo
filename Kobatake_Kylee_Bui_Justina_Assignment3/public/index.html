Meet the Founders

The store was founded in 2020 by up-and-coming coding designers, Justina and Kylee from the Shidler College of Business in Honolulu, HI. Leading with positivity is a driving force that the founders want to spread. In the busy lives we undergo, we often forget that we are loved. The Heart Depot supports mental health initiative programs that aim to help those in need.

The success of the business was made possible by their supporting family, friends, Professor Port, and loyal customers.
